# Privacy Policy

**RedNote Feed Time** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All processing happens locally within the browser. The extension:

- Decodes note IDs to timestamps entirely on your device
- Does not send any data to external servers
- Does not use cookies, tracking, or analytics
- Does not store any user information

## Data Collection Permissions

The extension declares **no data collection** in its Firefox manifest under `browser_specific_settings.gecko.data_collection_permissions`.
